Mass Exodus Fashion Show

On April 6th, 2006, The Mass Exodus Fashion show debuted LiveDescribe Technology. Vision impaired audience members were able to hear a detailed description of the show live or via a web cast. The show was also live captioned.

Hamlet Described

On November 25th, 2006 blind and vision impaired audience members attended a Live Described  Hart House Theatre production of Hamlet at the University of Toronto.  A touch tour was also organized prior to the event so that audience members had an an opportunity to experience sets, costumes and props.
